Welcome to my Computer Art Gallery
The Blueroom (1997) is inspired by my friend's  bedroom. Essentially, he lived in a barren boxroom that still had a sense of serenity.  He is a nomad with no belongings and somehow he seems at home with just a bed and window.

The Boxroom (1997) is a reinterpretation of the Blueroom.  It was a search for serenity within a perfect cube.  Most of the objects are taken from my belongings.   As I worked on this room, it became a collection of different things in my life, locked in a box. 

Jacob Rockowitz
